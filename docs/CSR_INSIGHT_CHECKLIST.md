# Clinical Study Report Insight Checklist

This checklist guides interpretation modules.

- Baseline comparability between treatment arms
- Direction and magnitude of change from baseline
- Time to peak and time to nadir
- Persistence versus transient abnormalities
- Outlier trajectories
- Treatment-emergent normal-to-high and normal-to-low shifts
- Laboratory parameters exceeding CTCAE or protocol thresholds
- Concordance across related biomarkers (e.g. ALT/AST/Bilirubin, eGFR/Creatinine/UACR)
- Separation of treatment arms over time
- Clinically meaningful versus statistically significant differences
- Visit windows with maximum treatment effect
- Safety signals requiring narrative discussion
- Candidate figures and tables suitable for CSR and publication
- Plain-language executive summary for clinicians and medical writers
