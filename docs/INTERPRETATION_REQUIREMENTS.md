# Interpretation Requirements

This document defines the target capabilities for the Interpretation module.

## Clinical summary
- Overall benefit-risk overview
- Arm-wise comparison of laboratory and vital sign trends
- Identification of clinically meaningful worsening or improvement
- Highlight parameters crossing normal ranges
- Highlight peak, nadir and abrupt changes
- Detect persistent versus transient abnormalities
- Summarize baseline imbalance
- Summarize treatment-emergent shifts
- Flag potential Hy's law, eGFR decline, QTc, liver safety and hematology signals when relevant data are available
- Provide plain-language summaries suitable for statisticians, clinicians, medical writers and KOL discussions

## Planned outputs
- Executive summary
- Parameter ranking
- Clinical signal table
- Interactive drill-down
- CSR-ready narrative suggestions
